How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. While initial improvements in technical aspects and local visibility can be seen within weeks, significant organic traffic and ranking improvements typically take 3-6 months, with continuous growth thereafter. The exact timeline depends on your industry, competition, and current website health.

What is technical SEO and why is it important?

Technical SEO focuses on optimizing your website's infrastructure to help search engine crawlers discover, crawl, and index your pages more effectively. This includes site speed, mobile-friendliness, structured data, canonicalization, and more. It's crucial because a technically sound website forms the foundation for all other SEO efforts, ensuring your content can actually be found and ranked.
