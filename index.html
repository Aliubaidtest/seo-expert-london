How quickly can I expect to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term investment. While some initial improvements in rankings and traffic can be seen within 3-6 months, significant, sustainable results typically manifest over 6-12 months or more. My strategies are designed for lasting impact, not quick fixes.

What is technical SEO and why is it important?

Technical SEO focuses on optimizing the technical aspects of your website to improve crawling, indexing, and overall search engine visibility. This includes site speed, mobile-friendliness, structured data, and security. It's crucial because a technically sound website forms the foundation for all other SEO efforts, ensuring search engines can effectively discover and rank your content.
