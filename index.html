Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEO and why is it important for my business?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results. It's crucial because it drives targeted visitors to your site, improves brand visibility, builds trust, and offers a strong return on investment compared to paid advertising.

How long does it take to see results from SEO efforts?

SEO is a long-term strategy. While some initial improvements can be seen within a few weeks (e.g., technical fixes, keyword ranking shifts), significant results often take 4-6 months, and sustained growth requires ongoing effort. Factors like industry competition and website history also play a role.
